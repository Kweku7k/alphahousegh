THE GRAMMY AND BET AWARD WINNING ARTISTE STONEBWOY SHUTS DOWN CU-SRC-WEEK PRIMUM'23

  • Ohene Mills
  • May 22nd,2023

Some of the Central University's dhope DJs opened the night with DJ TOTOCYCLINE , who wowed the crowd and warmed the house with the vibes. MC OJ showed Central University the love that he does so naturally, and of course, Before Stonebwoy graced the stage, the crowd were so hyped and ready for the BHIM President as performances from Central Students graced the stage to remind everyone that the we have talents right at home. Everyone should be unapologetic about that love.

Well, what are you expecting? ‘Bless His Imperial Majesty’, Stone came, and he gave the love as much as Central University gave him love. Now, if you’re unfamiliar with the “central Vibe”, let’s just say they are not an “easy” crowd as past shows have seen performers giving their all with students just staring back at them BUT not for Stonebwoy. The crowds showed up in their numbers from old skool BHIM natives to the new wave of young and diverse students who came with energy in full force for the President, singing his songs back to back in harmony with dancing to Stone. In the crowd, reppin and showing was Zigi and Alphahousegh The venue was packed, and all the air could say was ‘wow, Stonebwoy, what a guy, what a performer – outstanding.

Stonebwoy was joined on stage by Zigi, as they danced the night away who continued the celebrations because it was a Bhim Nation Movement. A very special day it was, which took place as the Forecourt of the cafeteria, Central University. History was served, and we were there to watch it happen.
