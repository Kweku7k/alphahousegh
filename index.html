About Alpha House

The focal aim of Alpha House marketing is to explore, identify, and project the real value and potential of any event or product.

  • Marketing: Alphahouse offers marketing services which are targeted at exposing the true value of product, where as finding the right audience for the right product.
  • Event Organizing: Event organization brings out the true nature of humans ie fun and creativity. Alphahouse Marketing boasts top notch quality and eye for details for the right moment and for the right project.
  • Fun Based activations: We believe that when people are happy they appreciate the value of activations and therefore get involved in the process. This causes the target market to be receptive of the product or activity.
  • Metrics and analytics: In Alphahouse we believe in the numbers and we believe the metrics speak and set the trend. We adopt research models that helps us serve our clients with tailored suit solutions.
